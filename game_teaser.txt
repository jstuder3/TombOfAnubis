Title: Tomb of Anubis

You are a group of explorers that found a new entrance to the Great Pyramid of Giza. 
Inside, you find a treasure. By taking it, you awaken Anubis from his slumber. 
You now have to find a way to lock him up again. 
Navigate a maze, dodge traps, use power-ups, cooperate and collect artefacts to entomb Anubis again.
